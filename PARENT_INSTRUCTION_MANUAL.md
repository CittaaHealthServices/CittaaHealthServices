# CITTAA Family Safety System - Parent Instruction Manual

## Welcome to CITTAA Family Safety

### What is CITTAA Family Safety?
CITTAA Family Safety is India's first transparent and compliant parental control system. Unlike traditional "invisible" monitoring, our system operates with complete transparency, ensuring your children understand and participate in their digital safety.

### Core Principles
- **Full Transparency:** Children know exactly what protection is active
- **Age-Appropriate Communication:** Safety explanations suitable for each child's age
- **Educational Approach:** Teaching digital citizenship alongside protection
- **DPDP Act 2023 Compliance:** Complete legal and regulatory adherence
- **Family Communication:** Open discussions about digital safety

## Getting Started

### System Access
**Website:** https://invisible-parental-control-app-5wfhkeb1.devinapps.com

### Step 1: Create Your Family Account

1. **Navigate to Registration**
   - Visit the CITTAA Family Safety website
   - Click "Create a family account"

2. **Complete Family Information**
   - **Family Name:** Enter your family name (e.g., "The Sharma Family")
   - **Your Full Name:** Enter the primary parent's name
   - **Email Address:** Use a valid email for account notifications
   - **Phone Number:** Optional, for emergency communications
   - **Password:** Create a secure password (minimum 8 characters)

3. **Compliance Commitments**
   Review and accept these essential commitments:
   - ✅ **Full transparency with children about all safety measures**
   - ✅ **Age-appropriate consent mechanisms for all features**
   - ✅ **Complete DPDP Act 2023 and children's privacy compliance**
   - ✅ **Educational approach to digital citizenship**

4. **Create Account**
   - Click "Create Family Account"
   - Check your email for verification (if required)

### Step 2: Initial Login and Setup

1. **Login to Your Dashboard**
   - Use your email and password to sign in
   - You'll see the main family dashboard

2. **Complete Family Profile**
   - Add family photo (optional)
   - Set family communication preferences
   - Configure notification settings

## Adding Children to Your Family

### Step 3: Add Child Profiles

1. **Navigate to Add Child**
   - Click "Add Child" from the main dashboard
   - Or go to "Family Management" → "Add New Child"

2. **Enter Child Information**
   - **Child's Name:** Full name of your child
   - **Age:** Current age (determines consent requirements)
   - **Grade/Class:** Educational level
   - **Interests:** Helps with educational content recommendations

3. **Age-Appropriate Consent Setup**

   **For Children Under 13:**
   - Parent provides full consent
   - Child receives age-appropriate explanation
   - Simple safety activation process

   **For Children 13-18:**
   - Parent consent required
   - Child must provide informed consent
   - Detailed explanation of data collection and protection

   **For Children 18+:**
   - Child provides own consent
   - Parent access limited to agreed monitoring
   - Full transparency and control

4. **Safety Profile Configuration**
   - **Protection Level:** Choose appropriate safety settings
   - **Educational Focus:** Select learning priorities
   - **Communication Style:** Set explanation preferences
   - **Monitoring Scope:** Define what activities to track

## Mobile Profile Management

Generate and manage mobile safety profiles for your children's devices:

### Generating Mobile Profiles

1. **Navigate to Mobile Profiles**: Click "Mobile Profiles" tab in dashboard or visit `/mobile-profiles`
2. **Select Child**: Choose which child needs a mobile profile
3. **Choose Device Type**: Select iOS or Android based on your child's device
4. **Generate Profile**: Click "Generate iOS Profile" or "Generate Android Profile"
5. **Copy Download URL**: Use the "Copy Download URL" button to get the secure link
6. **Share with Child**: Send the URL to your child or help them access it on their device

### Mobile Profile Features

Each mobile profile includes:
- **Transparent Content Filtering**: Blocks inappropriate content with educational explanations
- **Age-Appropriate Consent**: Proper consent mechanisms for children 13+
- **VPN Detection & Blocking**: Prevents circumvention of safety measures
- **Educational Content Promotion**: Promotes learning and digital citizenship
- **DPDP Act 2023 Compliance**: Full transparency and legal compliance

### Profile Download Process

1. **Child receives URL**: Share the secure download link with your child
2. **Profile Download**: Child accesses the URL on their mobile device
3. **Automatic Configuration**: Safety settings are automatically applied
4. **Transparent Setup**: Child sees clear explanations of all protection features
5. **Activation Confirmation**: System confirms profile is active and working

### Managing Existing Profiles

- **View Profile Status**: See which profiles are downloaded, activated, or pending
- **Copy URLs**: Re-copy download URLs for profiles not yet downloaded
- **Monitor Activity**: Track profile usage and safety feature effectiveness
- **Update Settings**: Modify profile configurations as children grow

### Troubleshooting Mobile Profiles

**Profile Not Downloading:**
- Ensure the download URL is accessed from the child's device
- Check internet connection on the mobile device
- Verify the URL hasn't expired (profiles expire after 7 days)

**Profile Not Activating:**
- Confirm child has followed all setup instructions
- Check that required permissions are granted on the device
- Contact support@cittaa.in if activation fails repeatedly
