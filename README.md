## About Us

CITTAA Health Services is a mental health technology company addressing India's critical shortage of psychological support in educational and healthcare institutions. We operate a hybrid B2B2C model that combines AI-powered voice analysis technology with qualified RCI-registered psychologists to deliver comprehensive mental wellness solutions.

### Our Mission

We are committed to bridging India's mental health treatment gap, where only 0.3 psychologists exist per 100,000 people versus the WHO-recommended 3 per 100,000. Through innovative technology and professional psychological services, we make mental health support accessible, culturally sensitive, and institutionally viable.

## Our Approach

### Hybrid Service Model
We combine the efficiency and scalability of AI technology with the empathy and expertise of qualified psychology professionals. Our RCI-registered psychologists work alongside our Vocalysis platform to provide:

- Early detection through AI-powered screening
- Professional intervention by qualified psychologists
- Continuous monitoring and follow-up support
- Crisis response protocols compliant with POCSO Act

### Institutional Partnerships
Our services are designed specifically for Indian educational and healthcare institutions:

- **Schools:** Full-time placements, roving psychology services, student wellness programs
- **Hospitals:** Department-integrated mental health services, patient support programs
- **Government:** Policy-aligned programs supporting state mental health initiatives
- **Corporates:** Employee wellness, stress management, workplace mental health
