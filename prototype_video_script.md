## TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS FOR VIDEO PRODUCTION

### Video Requirements:
- **Resolution:** 4K (3840x2160) for professional presentation
- **Format:** MP4 with H.264 encoding
- **Audio:** High-quality narration with background music
- **Duration:** 8-10 minutes optimal for investor attention
- **Subtitles:** English and Hindi for accessibility

### Screen Recording Specifications:
- **Web Dashboard:** 1920x1080 browser recordings
- **Mobile Mockups:** High-resolution PNG exports
- **System Architecture:** Professional diagram animations
- **Compliance Documentation:** Clean document presentations

### Production Notes:
- Use professional voiceover talent
- Include CITTAA branding throughout
- Maintain consistent visual style
- Ensure all text is readable at presentation size
- Include contact information and QR codes for follow-up

### Delivery Formats:
- **Master File:** 4K MP4 for high-quality presentations
- **Presentation Version:** 1080p MP4 for standard displays
- **Social Media Version:** 720p MP4 with captions
- **Mobile Version:** Optimized for smartphone viewing
